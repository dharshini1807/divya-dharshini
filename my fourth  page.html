    The Med Time feature is designed to help users take their medicines on time without forgetting. This feature allows users to set a specific time for their medication, and it gives an alarm or reminder at the scheduled time. Med Time is created to support a healthy routine by ensuring medicines are taken regularly and on time. It is especially useful for people who have a busy schedule or need timely reminders. This feature was developed as part of my project to understand how time-based reminders work in web applications. It makes the application more useful and user-friendly by providing timely alerts.
