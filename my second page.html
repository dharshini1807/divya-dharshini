Morning Routine:

  1. Wake up early – Aim for 6–7 AM.
  2. Hydrate – Drink a glass of water.
  3. Stretch / Exercise – 10–20 minutes of yoga, jogging, or simple stretches.
  4. Healthy Breakfast – Include protein, fruits, and whole grains.
  5. Plan your day – Write down top 3 priorities in your to-do list.

Daytime routine:

  1. Focus sessions – Use time blocks for work or study.
  2. Healthy snack – Fruits, nuts, or yogurt.
  3. Hydrate regularly – Drink water every 1–2 hours.
  4. Short breaks – Take 5–10 minutes every hour to walk/stretch.
  5. Sunlight / Fresh air – Spend at least 10–15 minutes outdoors.

Afternoon Routine:

  1. Balanced lunch – Include vegetables, protein, and complex carbs.
  2. Power nap / Relaxation – 10–20 minutes if needed.
  3. Check progress – Tick off completed tasks.

Evening Routine:

  1. Exercise / Walk – Light activity to refresh the mind.
  2. Healthy dinner – Light meal, avoid heavy carbs.
  3. Reflect on the day – Gratitude journal or note what went well.
  4. Digital detox – Reduce phone/computer usage 1 hour before bed.

Night Routine:

  1. Prepare for next day – Plan tasks, set reminders.
  2. Relaxation – Reading, meditation, or listening to calm music.
  3. Sleep – Aim for 7–8 hours of restful sleep.
